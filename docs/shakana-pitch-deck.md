## SLIDE 1 — WHO WE ARE

**Shakana** is Israel's first social group-buying platform built for apartment buildings and neighborhoods.

We are a small, fast-moving team building the infrastructure for neighbors to shop together, split shipping costs, and unlock group discounts — directly from their phones.

---

## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

Israelis overpay for shipping every day.

- A neighbor in 3B orders the same product from the same store as the neighbor in 5A.
- They each pay ₪35–₪60 shipping separately.
- They don't know the other person even exists.

**This happens millions of times per month across Israel.**

Stores want volume. Customers want savings. Nobody connects them.

Until now.

---

## SLIDE 3 — WHAT WE DO

Shakana lets one person in a building start a group order.

**The flow:**
1. User finds a product they want
2. Opens a group → shares a link to the building WhatsApp group
3. Neighbors join, pick their size/color/quantity
4. Timer counts down
5. One order. One delivery. One address.
6. Everyone saves.

**No logistics company. No warehouse. No middleman.**

We are the coordination layer between neighbors and stores.

## SLIDE 6 — PAYMENT MODEL

We offer two payment flows:

---

### 💳 OPTION 1 — BIT FLOW (Zero Friction)

**Best for:** early adopters, stores who want simplicity

**How it works:**
- Each participant sees their total in-app
- App generates a personal Bit payment link for each user
- Payment goes directly to the store (or to the group founder who transfers)
- Once all payments confirmed → order placed

**Pros:** Israelis already use Bit. No credit card needed. Instant.
**Cons:** Requires coordination. Slight manual step.

---

### 🔒 OPTION 2 — PREPAY & AUTO-ORDER (Full Product)

**Best for:** scale, investor story, full automation

**How it works:**
1. User joins group → selects product (size, color, quantity)
2. User pays immediately via credit card / Apple Pay / Google Pay
3. Timer counts down (24h / 48h / custom)
4. When timer hits zero → order placed **automatically** to the founder's address
5. No human action needed. No chasing people for payment.
6. If group doesn't reach minimum → full refunds issued automatically

**The founder gets:** a small coordinator bonus (2% extra discount or store credit)

**Pros:** fully automated, scales infinitely, clean investor story
**Cons:** requires payment infrastructure (Stripe) — already built ✓

**This is the model we are building toward.**
